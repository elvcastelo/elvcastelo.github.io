Emanuel Castelo — Profile

Short bio

I am a PhD student at Aix‑Marseille Université under the supervision of Jérémie Chalopin and co-supervision of Oscar Defrain.

During my master's at Universidade Federal do Ceará, as a member of the ParGO research group, I wrote a thesis on valid inequalities for the k‑Color Shortest Path problem. See my DBLP and Google Scholar entries.

Research interests: Generation Algorithms, Computational Complexity Theory, Combinatorial Optimization, and their conical combination.
