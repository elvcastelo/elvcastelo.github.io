Short Bio

I am a PhD student at Aix-Marseille Université under supervision of Jérémie Chalopin and co-supervision of Oscar Defrain.

During my master's at Universidade Federal do Ceará, as a member of the ParGO research group, I wrote a thesis on valid inequalities for the k-Color Shortest Path problem. I also have a law degree, for whatever reason*. See also my dblp and Google Scholar entries.

Research interests: Enumeration Algorithms**, Computational Complexity Theory, and everything in-between.

Contact: emanuel.castelo[at]lis-lab.fr

Office: Aix-Marseille University, Campus de Luminy, Laboratoire d'Informatique et Systèmes, TPR2, 5th Floor, Office 05.24.
