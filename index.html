I am a PhD student at Aix‑Marseille Université under the supervision of Jérémie Chalopin and co-supervision of Oscar Defrain. In my thesis, we aim at obtaining efficient algorithms for enumeration problems. See also my DBLP and Google Scholar entries.

Research interests: Generation Algorithms, Computational Complexity Theory, Combinatorial Optimization, and their conical combination.
